## âš ï¸ Potential Risks

### Risk 1: Misuse for Replacement

**Scenario**: Organization uses SPAR-Kit to centralize decisions, removing distributed decision-making roles.

**Mitigation**:
- SPAR-Kit architecture requires human approval
- Audit trail creates accountability
- [Responsible Deployment Guidelines](./RESPONSIBLE_DEPLOYMENT.md) prohibit this use

### Risk 2: Deskilling

**Scenario**: Workers become dependent on AI and lose decision-making capabilities.

**Mitigation**:
- Override mechanisms require active judgment
- Low-confidence recommendations mandate human analysis
- [Upskilling Pathways](./UPSKILLING_PATHWAYS.md) maintain skill development

### Risk 3: Surveillance Misuse

**Scenario**: Organization uses session data to monitor employee decisions.

**Mitigation**:
- Local-first architecture
- Session data owned by user
- [Data Privacy Standards](./DATA_PRIVACY.md) prohibit surveillance use
