## ğŸ¢ Responsible Deployment Guidelines

### For Organizations

1. **Position as enhancement**
   - "SPAR-Kit helps you make better decisions"
   - NOT "SPAR-Kit makes decisions for you"

2. **Invest in training**
   - Teach effective persona selection
   - Train on confidence interpretation
   - Practice override judgment

3. **Measure quality, not speed**
   - Track decision outcomes, not decisions/hour
   - Reward thoughtful overrides
   - Value documented reasoning

4. **Maintain human authority**
   - Require human sign-off
   - Document override rationale
   - Review AI recommendations critically

### For Individuals

1. **Develop AI collaboration skills**
   - Learn effective prompting
   - Understand confidence scores
   - Practice override judgment

2. **Strengthen uniquely human skills**
   - Relationship building
   - Ethical reasoning
   - Contextual judgment
   - Creative problem-solving

3. **Stay in the loop**
   - Always review AI recommendations
   - Question unexpected outputs
   - Trust your domain expertise
