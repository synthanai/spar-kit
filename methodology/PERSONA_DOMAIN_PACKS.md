## How to Use Preset Packs

1. **Identify your decision type** from the categories below
2. **Copy all 4-6 personas** from the relevant pack
3. **Follow the SPARKIT Protocol** (Scope ‚Üí Populate ‚Üí Announce ‚Üí Rumble ‚Üí Knit ‚Üí Interrogate ‚Üí Transmit)
4. **Customize as needed** ‚Äî these are starting points, not constraints


## Creating Custom Packs

**Rules for good pack design:**

1. **Include at least one tension pair** (P‚ÜîE, R‚ÜîN, or S‚ÜîA)
2. **Keep to 4-6 personas** for cognitive manageability
3. **Include at least one Observer** for meta-awareness on complex decisions
4. **Customize persona names** for domain relevance (e.g., "Student Advocate" instead of "User Advocate")
5. **Test the pack** on a real decision before standardizing
