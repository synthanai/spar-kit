# RAMP Levels: Decision Gravity Assessment

*Five levels for calibrating governance rigor to decision stakes.*

> **Version**: 1.0 (Protocol v8.0)
> **Acronym**: Reversibility, Accountability, Measurability, Proportionality

---

## Overview

Not every decision needs the same governance. RAMP provides a 5-level assessment that determines how much rigor to apply during INTERROGATE and TRANSMIT. The higher the RAMP level, the more gates a synthesis must clear before becoming a verdict.

---

## The Five Levels

| Level | Name | Stakes | Governance |
|-------|------|--------|------------|
| **L1** | Routine | Low stakes, easily reversible | Lightweight |
| **L2** | Operational | Moderate stakes, short-term impact | Standard |
| **L3** | Strategic | Significant investment, medium-term | Full protocol |
| **L4** | Transformational | Major commitment, hard to reverse | Enhanced scrutiny |
| **L5** | Existential | Identity-defining, irreversible | Maximum rigor |

---

## RAMP Assessment Criteria

Score each dimension (1-5):

| Dimension | L1 (Low) | L3 (Medium) | L5 (High) |
|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------|
| **R**eversibility | Easily undone | Costly to reverse | Irreversible |
| **A**ccountability | Self only | Team/department | Organization/public |
| **M**easurability | Clear metrics exist | Mixed signals | Unmeasurable for years |
| **P**roportionality | Fits within existing authority | Stretches mandate | Exceeds current scope |

**RAMP Level** = Average of four scores, rounded to nearest level.

---

## Governance by RAMP Level

### INTERROGATE Phase Gates

| Gate | L1-L2 | L3 | L4-L5 |
|------|-------|----|-------|
| PROBE Questions | 1 | 3 | 5 |
| Pre-mortem | Skip | Each persona | Each + external |
| ASPIRES Check | Abbreviated | Full 7-point | Full + written rationale |

### TRANSMIT Phase Requirements

| Requirement | L1-L2 | L3 | L4-L5 |
|-------------|-------|----|-------|
| Confidence threshold | > 50% | > 65% | > 80% |
| Dissent record | Optional | Required | Required + named |
| Conditions | Optional | Required | Required + triggers |
| Review timeline | None | Quarterly | Monthly |

## Examples

| Decision | R | A | M | P | Level |
|----------|---|---|---|---|-------|
| "Should we use React or Vue?" | 2 | 1 | 3 | 1 | **L2** |
| "Should we enter the Singapore market?" | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3 | **L3** |
| "Should we acquire Company X?" | 4 | 5 | 4 | 4 | **L4** |
| "Should we pivot from B2B to B2C?" | 5 | 5 | 5 | 5 | **L5** |

---

## Integration with Depth Modes

RAMP levels and depth modes are related but independent:

| RAMP Level | Recommended Depth Mode |
|------------|----------------------|
| L1 | ⚡ Quick |
| L2 | ⚖️ Basic |
| L3 | 💣 Stress Test |
| L4 | 🧠 Deep |
| L5 | 🤯 Ultra |

You *can* run an Ultra debate on an L1 decision (if you want to practice), but you should *never* run a Quick debate on an L5 decision.
