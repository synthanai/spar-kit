# SPARED: The Coaching Cycle

*Five phases for developing decision-making capacity through structured guidance.*

> **Version**: 1.0 (Protocol v8.0)
> **Acronym**: SPAR Emergence Design

---

## Overview

SPARED is the pedagogical edition of SPAR, designed for adaptive learning. It maps a coaching journey through five phases, from grounding through integration. Each phase corresponds to a natural element and a mode of cognitive engagement.

Where SPARKIT is the *tool*, SPARED is the *curriculum*.

---

## The Five Phases

| Phase | Element | Action | Cognitive Mode | Protocol Mapping |
|-------|---------|--------|---------------|------------------|
| 🌍 **GROUND** | Earth | Still the mind, name the tension | Inquiry | SCOPE, POPULATE |
| 💧 **FLOW** | Water | Steelman the opposing view | Provocative | ABSTRACT |
| 🔥 **BURN** | Fire | Propose a transcendent third | Creative | RUMBLE, KNIT |
| 💨 **EXPAND** | Air | Facilitate, see the system | Socratic | INTERROGATE |
| ✨ **RETURN** | Space | Integrate into concrete action | Direct | TRANSMIT |

---

## Phase Details

### 🌍 GROUND

**"Before you debate, still yourself."**

The practitioner:
- Names the core tension (what two values are in conflict?)
- Identifies their current lean (where do they instinctively fall?)
- Surfaces anxiety (what are they afraid of discovering?)

This maps to SCOPE and POPULATE: defining the question and selecting the voices that will challenge it.

### 💧 FLOW

**"Before you attack, strengthen."**

The practitioner:
- Takes the position they most disagree with
- Builds the strongest possible case FOR that position
- Identifies what that position sees that their own position misses

This maps to ABSTRACT: building the cognitive map requires genuine engagement with opposing frames.

### 🔥 BURN

**"The collision creates something new."**

The practitioner:
- Allows positions to collide without resolving
- Seeks the "third option" that neither side proposed
- Notices what emerges from the friction

This maps to RUMBLE and KNIT: the dialectic produces synthesis, not compromise.

### 💨 EXPAND

**"Step back. See the whole system."**

The practitioner:
- Facilitates rather than argues
- Identifies which voices were underweighted
- Questions the process itself (PROBE stance)

This maps to INTERROGATE: stress-testing the synthesis from a systems perspective.

### ✨ RETURN

**"Bring it home. What will you actually do?"**

The practitioner:
- Converts insight into specific action
- Names what they learned
- Commits to one concrete next step

This maps to TRANSMIT: the verdict must be actionable, not abstract.

## Progression Levels

SPARED supports three entry points based on practitioner experience:

| Level | Description | SPARKIT Equivalent |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| 🟢 **Gentle Entry** | Single perspective introduced | Show Me depth mode |
| 🟡 **Standard** | Full 4-direction compass | Basic to Stress Test |
| 🔴 **Deep** | Full 8-step with 8+ personas | Deep to Ultra |

### Entry Protocol (3 Questions)

1. **Selection**: "What decision are you facing?"
2. **Hardship**: "What makes this decision hard for you personally?"
3. **Depth**: "How deep do you want to go?"

## SPARED as Personal Practice

SPARED works as a solo practice (STASH mode: Solo Human):

```
Weekly SPARED ritual:
1. GROUND (5 min): Name the biggest tension from this week
2. FLOW (10 min): Write the case for the option you rejected
3. BURN (10 min): What third option exists that you haven't considered?
4. EXPAND (5 min): What pattern does this decision reveal about how you decide?
5. RETURN (5 min): One specific action for next week
```
