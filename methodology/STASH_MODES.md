## What is STASH?

**STASH** defines the five ways to run SPAR. The methodology is mode-agnostic â€” the same SPARKIT protocol, GRACE principles, and ASPIRES patterns work across all modes. STASH simply describes *who* embodies the personas.

| Letter | Mode | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| **S** | **Solo Human** | One person role-plays multiple personas |
| **T** | **Team Human** | Team members each embody assigned personas |
| **A** | **AI-Persona** | Multiple LLM instances argue with each other |
| **S** | **Solo AI** | One LLM role-plays multiple personas |
| **H** | **Hybrid** | Mix of AI personas and human participants |

### ðŸ§  Foundational Principle: Human Modes Are Primary

> **Solo Human and Team Human modes are the foundation of SPAR.**  
> AI modes are powerful augmentation tools, not the origin of the practice.

The wisdom comes from *structured disagreement*, not from AI. SPAR was designed as a human cognitive practice first. AI simply allows:
- **Speed** â€” Generate 4+ perspectives in minutes
- **Breadth** â€” Access personas outside your experience
- **Tirelessness** â€” Debate for hours without fatigue

But AI cannot replace:
- **Tacit knowledge** â€” What you know that you don't know you know
- **Accountability** â€” Real skin in the game
- **Relational trust** â€” The psychological safety of team deliberation
- **Presence** â€” The embodied experience of inhabiting a perspective

**Start with human modes. Add AI as it becomes useful.**

> *"The philosopher's practice â€” building your own capacity for multi-perspectival thinking."*

## When to Use

- Personal decisions that affect your life
- Building perspective-taking capacity over time
- When you need deep reflection, not speed
- When external input isn't available or appropriate

## How It Works

You inhabit each persona sequentially, arguing from their worldview as if it were your own. The value isn't the output â€” it's the practice of genuinely holding contradictory positions.

## The Practice

1. **Choose 3-4 personas** with genuinely conflicting worldviews
2. **Set the scene** â€” State the decision clearly
3. **Embody each persona** â€” Write or speak from their perspective
4. **Physically shift** â€” Change position, location, or writing instrument between personas
5. **Synthesize** â€” Step back as "moderator" and notice tensions

## Mode Selection Matrix

| Your Situation | Recommended Mode | Why |
|----------------|------------------|-----|
| Personal life decision | Solo Human (S) | Build your own capacity |
| Quick business exploration | Solo AI (S) | Speed, low cost |
| Pre-meeting preparation | AI-Persona (A) | Thorough exploration |
| Team strategic planning | Team Human (T) | Build alignment |
| Board-level decision | Hybrid (H) | AI breadth + human wisdom |
| Training / Development | Team Human (T) | Skill building |
| 24/7 decision support | AI-Persona (A) | Always available |
| Deadline pressure | Solo AI (S) | Fastest option |
| Multi-stakeholder complexity | Hybrid (H) | Most perspectives |
| Building organizational culture | Team Human (T) | Embeds the practice |

# Integration with Core SPAR Frameworks

All five STASH modes use the same core frameworks:

| Framework | What It Is | Used In |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| **Four Directions (N-E-W-S)** | Canonical 4-persona model | All modes |
| **SPARKIT Protocol (7 steps)** | Debate structure | All modes |
| **GRACE Principles (5 stances)** | Non-negotiables | All modes |
| **ASPIRES Framework (7 patterns)** | Advanced techniques | All modes |
| **109-Persona Library** | Extended personas | All modes |

The frameworks are mode-agnostic. Whether a persona is embodied by a human, an AI, or both â€” the same principles apply.
